🛡️ Stay Safe Online: Student Checklist

1. PIN Logic: Never enter your UPI PIN to receive money. A PIN is only required to send money.

2. Official Apps: Only use official banking or UPI apps from the Play Store/App Store.

3. Two-Factor Authentication (2FA): Enable 2FA on all social media and email accounts.

4. Public Wi-Fi: Avoid logging into bank accounts or making payments while connected to free public Wi-Fi.

5. Link Safety: Do not click on "urgent" links in SMS or emails claiming your account is blocked.

6. Privacy Settings: Keep your social media profiles private to prevent identity theft.

7. Verify Sellers: Before scanning any QR code for a transaction, verify the identity of the person you are dealing with.

8. Password Hygiene: Use a mix of symbols, numbers, and letters; never use your birthdate or "1234".

🚨 How to Report Cybercrime in India
If you are a victim of fraud, act immediately:

1. Portal: Visit cybercrime.gov.in

2. Helpline: Call 1930 (National Cybercrime Helpline Number)
